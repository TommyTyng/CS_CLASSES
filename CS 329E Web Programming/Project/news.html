Breaking News in the Music Community

Spotify Supports Mental Health Through Mint Playlist Merch Line

Tcfghe largest playlist in the world for dance and electronic music is the mint playlist from Spotify and they recently just hit a milestone, breaking 5 million followers. To celebrate such a huge achievement they launched their first ever line of mint branded merchandise. They put a little more thought into this and actually partnered up with the incredible To Write Loves On Her Arms organization and all proceeds are going to be supporting mental health. Austin Kramer, Spotify Global Dance & Electronic programmer, is not only the person behind curating the mint playlist, but was the driving force of this initiative. This is what he had to say about it.

Tyler The Creator's Camp Flog Gnaw Festival Drops Stacked 2018 Lineup Ft. Kids See Ghosts

Tyler, The Creator’s Camp Flog Gnaw festival brings an incredible lineup every year, and in a time when every festival looks the same, his manages to stand out with an incredible lineup. The obvious highlight here is the first announced live performance of Kanye West and Kid Cudi’s Kids See Ghosts project as well as an absolutely stacked lineup. This year it takes places at Dodger Stadium Nov. 10-11 - head to their website here for more info. Headliners for the festival include Tyler, The Creator, A$AP Rocky, Post Malone, SZA and Ms. Lauryn Hill. From there, it doesn’t drop off at all… there’s Pusha T, Billie Eilish, Jorja Smith, BROCKHAMPTON, The Internet, Kali Uchis, Jaden Smith, Earl Sweatshirt, Rex Orange County, Flatbush Zombies, Majid Jordan, Little Dragon, Virgil Abloh and more. This has some of the best hip-hop, soul and indie mix we’ve seen and definitely want to make it this year. Enjoy!

Chicago Rapper Mick Jenkins Drops Hard-Hitting New Song "Bruce Banner" & Announces Forthcoming Albummi

Mick Jenkins just gave us some amazing news… along with a fire new track. The Chicago rapper has just shared the news his new album is coming soon as his first full album since his 2016 project The Healing Component. Along with this, he’s delivered a hard-hitting new song “Bruce Banner.” While his last project was soulful, this new song is dark and just filled with straight bars as he calls out his competition saying “honestly can’t nobody come for me except Kendrick.” This new track comes with an eerie beat while we hear Mick go in for lyrical verses changing his vocal inflections throughout, even sounding like Aesop Rock at moments. He puts his impressive vocal dexterity on full display, skipping along the hard-hitting beat. This is more aggressive than we’ve hear Mick on a lot of his raps and can’t wait to hear what else is in store. Enjoy!
